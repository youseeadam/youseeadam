## 🎬 My Career Journey: From the Stage to the Server Room

I've spent most of my career in IT and tech, but I took a pretty unconventional route to get here:

* 🎭 **The Early Days:** Armed with a BFA and MFA in Performing Arts Design Technology and Management, I worked on movies, TV shows, and even did some work for Imagineering! But honestly, live theater was always the most fun.
* 🌐 **The '90s Web Boom:** I co-founded a company doing commercials and websites. My business partner wanted to lean into commercials, but I decided to bet on this up-and-coming thing called the "Internet."
* 💾 **The IT Trenches:** I jumped fully into IT in the late '90s and have survived *every single* Microsoft Exchange migration from 4.0 all the way through Exchange Online to M365. 
* 🎤 **Finding My Voice at Aruba Networks:** I helped lead a massive "cut the cord" project, moving everything to IP/Softphones. Presenting this project to different groups sparked my absolute passion for Technical Marketing.
* 📚 **The UC Consulting Era:** I spent years consulting and deploying OCS, Lync, Skype for Business, and Teams. I even co-authored a couple of chapters for *Skype for Business Unleashed*.
* 📹 **The Logitech Years (Present):** When I had the opportunity to work for Logitech 7 years ago, I couldn't pass it up. I work in the Alliances group as the "Everything Else Guy." If we aren't sure how a partner fits into the Logitech Video Collaboration portfolio, I figure it out. I spend most of my time with API and Display Partners, and I'm proudly known as the "Windows PC Guy" (I build our Teams and Zoom room images!).
